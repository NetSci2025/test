# NetSci 2025

NetSci, the International School and Conference on Network Science, is the flagship conference on Complex Networks promoted by the Network Science Society. It brings under one umbrella a wide variety of leading researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders with a direct interest in **Network Science**, from Physics to Computer Science, Biology, Social Sciences, Economics, and Technological and Communication Networks, among others.
